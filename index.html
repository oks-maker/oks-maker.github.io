About Us

At All Set, we believe every sport has unique demands and hydration should rise to meet them. Most sports drinks take a one-size-fits-all approach, but a swimmer’s needs differ from a runner’s, just as a basketball player’s fueling demands differ from a football player’s.

That’s why we developed our formulas in collaboration with experts in sports nutrition and tested them directly with athletes. Each mix balances electrolytes, carbohydrates, and hydration support in ratios tailored to performance and recovery for specific activities.

Our products are produced in licensed, compliant commercial kitchens here in Austin, Texas, using high-quality ingredients you can trust. From packaging to production, we’re committed to transparency, sustainability, and the science of helping athletes perform at their best.

Whether you’re training for your first triathlon, stepping on the field, or simply moving through your day, All Set is designed to keep you fueled, hydrated, and ready.

About Me

I’m Olivia Kate Stephens, the founder of All Set. As an Ironman triathlete and lifelong athlete, I know firsthand how important hydration is to training and competition. I’ve spent years experimenting with fueling strategies during races, and I noticed a gap: most products are one-size-fits-all. Athletes shouldn't be forced to cobble together 3-9 different products in 2025.

My goal with All Set is simple: to create hydration that matches the unique demands of different sports, and to do it in a way that’s transparent, practical, and trustworthy. When I’m not building All Set, you’ll probably find me swimming laps, cycling Austin’s hills, or exploring new trails.
