About Us

At All Set, we believe athletes and active people deserve more than generic sports drinks. Every sport places different demands on the body, and hydration should be tailored to meet those needs. That’s why we created a line of single-serve hydration mixes designed for real performance, recovery, and everyday movement.

Our formulas are built on simplicity and effectiveness: clean electrolytes, balanced carbohydrates, and flavors that keep you reaching for your bottle. Produced in licensed commercial kitchens here in Austin, All Set is committed to quality, compliance, and sustainability. We’re here to help you stay fueled, focused, and ready for whatever comes next.

About Me

I’m Olivia Kate Stephens, the founder of All Set. As an Ironman triathlete and lifelong athlete, I know firsthand how important hydration is to training and competition. I’ve spent years experimenting with fueling strategies during races, and I noticed a gap: most products are one-size-fits-all.

My goal with All Set is simple: to create hydration that matches the unique demands of different sports, and to do it in a way that’s transparent, practical, and trustworthy. When I’m not building All Set, you’ll probably find me swimming laps, cycling Austin’s hills, or exploring new trails.
